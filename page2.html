Take a Moment

Pause. Relax. Breathe deeply and let the world fade for a while.

A Gentle Breathing Guide


Sometimes our minds move too quickly and our bodies forget to slow down. Taking a few moments to breathe deeply can help calm your thoughts and bring your focus back to the present moment.


Follow this simple rhythm while watching the video:

  • Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 seconds.
  • Hold the breath gently for 4 seconds.
  • Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds.
  • Repeat the cycle for a few minutes.

There is no need to rush. Let each breath be soft and natural. Allow your shoulders to relax and your thoughts to pass like clouds. This small pause can make a big difference in your day.
